About Us

The Art Enthusiasts Club is a space for creative minds to come together and share their passion for art. Our aim is to nurture talent, provide a platform for artistic expression, and foster an environment where students can explore their creativity in various forms like painting, sketching, digital art, and sculpture. Our mission is to inspire creativity and spread awareness of different art forms.
